Step 03 · Methodology

How the prediction engine works

A hybrid approach combining structured regression features with unstructured sentiment signals.

01

Feature Engineering

Sqft, BHK, bathrooms, property age, floor level and locality tier are normalized into model-ready inputs.

02

Sentiment Analysis (NLP)

Buyer reviews, news headlines and social chatter are scored using a VADER/Transformer-style sentiment pipeline, producing a bullish-to-bearish index.

03

Weighted Regression

A gradient-boosted regression model blends structured features with the sentiment multiplier to output a final INR estimate.

04

Confidence Scoring

Prediction intervals are derived from residual variance across comparable listings in the training set.
